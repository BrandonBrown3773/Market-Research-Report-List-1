<p><strong>The Estradiol Test Kit Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Time-resolved Immunofluorescence</li><li>Chemiluminescence Immunoassay</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The Estradiol Test Kit market is primarily categorized into two types: Time-resolved Immunofluorescence (TRIF) and Chemiluminescence Immunoassay (CLIA). TRIF utilizes fluorescent labels and time-resolved detection to enhance sensitivity and specificity in measuring estradiol levels in samples. In contrast, CLIA employs chemiluminescent labels that produce light when a chemical reaction occurs, enabling precise quantification of estradiol. Both methods are vital for diagnostics in reproductive health and hormone regulation, appealing to healthcare providers for their accuracy and reliability.</p></p>
