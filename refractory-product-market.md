<p><p>The refractory product market is categorized into two main types: clay and non-clay refractories. Clay refractories, primarily composed of alumino-silicate, are known for their high thermal resistance and are commonly used in kilns and furnaces. Non-clay refractories include products made from materials like zirconia and silicon carbide, which offer superior performance in extreme conditions. These non-clay options are often utilized in industries requiring enhanced durability and thermal stability, such as metallurgy and petrochemicals, thereby catering to diverse industrial needs.</p></p>
