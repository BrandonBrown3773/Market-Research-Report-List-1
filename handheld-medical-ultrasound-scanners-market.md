<p><p>The handheld medical ultrasound scanners market is divided into two main types based on display technology: black & white and color display. Black & white ultrasound scanners primarily present images in grayscale, which are effective for a variety of diagnostic purposes and are typically more affordable. In contrast, color display ultrasound scanners provide real-time color Doppler imaging, allowing for enhanced visualization of blood flow and anatomical details. This feature is particularly beneficial in vascular and obstetric applications, improving diagnostic accuracy.</p></p>
