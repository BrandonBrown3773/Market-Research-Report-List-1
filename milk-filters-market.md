<p><p>The milk filters market consists of three primary types: metal milk filters, fabric milk filters, and polypropylene milk filters. Metal milk filters are durable and reusable, ideal for efficient filtration. Fabric milk filters, often made from cotton or synthetic materials, offer good filtration and are typically disposable, making them convenient for small-scale operations. Polypropylene milk filters are lightweight, chemically resistant, and suitable for various processing conditions, providing effective filtration with easy disposal options. Each type serves specific needs in the dairy industry.</p></p>
