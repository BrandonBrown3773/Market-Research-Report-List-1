<p><ul><li>Lumped Type</li><li>Discrete Type</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Fiber Raman Amplifiers (FRAs) utilize the Raman scattering effect to amplify optical signals in fiber optic communications. The market consists of two main types: Lumped Type and Discrete Type. Lumped Type FRAs provide amplification at specific intervals along the fiber, enhancing overall signal strength in a compact configuration. In contrast, Discrete Type FRAs offer amplification at distinct points, allowing for more flexibility and targeted enhancement in various network designs. Both types are vital for improving the performance of optical networks.</p></p>
