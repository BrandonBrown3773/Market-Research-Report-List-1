<p><p>The automotive inlet manifolds market is categorized into three main types based on material: aluminum, cast iron, and composite plastic. Aluminum inlet manifolds are lightweight and offer superior thermal conductivity, enhancing engine performance. Cast iron manifolds are durable and resistant to high temperatures but are heavier. Composite plastic materials provide corrosion resistance and can be molded into complex shapes, making them lightweight and cost-effective. Each material type serves specific needs in performance, weight, and production efficiency for automotive applications.</p></p>
