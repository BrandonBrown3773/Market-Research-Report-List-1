<p><p>The gel electrophoresis apparatus market includes various types such as agarose gel, pulse field gel, and temperature gradient gel. Agarose gel electrophoresis is commonly used for separating nucleic acids and proteins based on size. Pulse field gel electrophoresis allows for the separation of larger DNA fragments by altering the electric field's direction. Temperature gradient gel electrophoresis employs varying temperatures to facilitate the separation of biomolecules based on their properties. Each type serves specific research and diagnostic applications in molecular biology.</p></p>
