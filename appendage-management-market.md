Epicardial LAA closure devices are placed on the outer surface of the heart, offering a minimally invasive approach to reduce stroke risk in patients with atrial fibrillation. In contrast, endocardial devices are inserted into the heart’s interior through catheters, providing a less invasive solution for LAA closure. Both types aim to prevent thrombus formation and enhance patient outcomes in managing heart-related conditions.</p></p>
