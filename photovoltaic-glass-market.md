<p><p>The photovoltaic glass market consists of three main types: Poly-Crystalline, Mono-Crystalline, and Thin Film. Poly-Crystalline panels are made from multiple silicon crystals, offering a balance of efficiency and cost. Mono-Crystalline panels, created from single silicon crystals, provide higher efficiency and space efficiency but tend to be pricier. Thin Film technology involves a lightweight, flexible layer of materials, making it suitable for various applications but generally with lower efficiency compared to crystalline options. Each type caters to different performance and installation needs.</p></p>
