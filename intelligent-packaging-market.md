<p><p>The intelligent packaging market consists of three main types: mechanical, electronic, and electric packaging. Electronic type packaging incorporates advanced technologies such as RFID and QR codes to track and communicate information about the product, improving supply chain efficiency. Electric type packaging involves systems that actively respond to environmental changes, such as temperature control, ensuring optimal preservation and product integrity during transportation and storage.</p></p>
