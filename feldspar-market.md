<p><p>The feldspar market is segmented primarily into three types: Orthoclase, Plagioclase, and Others. Orthoclase, a potassium-rich feldspar, is widely used in ceramics and glass production due to its ability to enhance strength and durability. Plagioclase, composed of sodium and calcium, is essential in the manufacturing of glass and pottery, contributing to their aesthetic and structural properties. The "Others" category includes various feldspar types utilized in specialized applications, such as fillers in plastics and paints, highlighting the material's versatility.</p></p>
