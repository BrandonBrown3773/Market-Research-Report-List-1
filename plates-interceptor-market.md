<p><strong>Market Segmentation</strong></p>
<p><strong>The Plates Interceptor Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>CPI (Corrugated Plate Interceptor)</li><li>TPI (Tilted Plate Interceptor)</li><li>PPI (Parallel Plate Interceptor)</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The Plates Interceptor market encompasses three primary types designed for effective separation of suspended solids and grease from water. The Corrugated Plate Interceptor (CPI) features a corrugated plate design to enhance sedimentation efficiency. The Tilted Plate Interceptor (TPI) utilizes angled plates to facilitate faster settlement of particles. The Parallel Plate Interceptor (PPI) employs flat plates arranged parallel to improve flow dynamics and separation efficiency. Each type serves different applications in wastewater treatment and stormwater management, optimizing environmental compliance.</p></p>
