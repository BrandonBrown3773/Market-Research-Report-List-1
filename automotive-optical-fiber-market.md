<p><strong>The Automotive Optical Fiber Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Multimode Fiber</li><li>Single-Mode Fiber</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The automotive optical fiber market consists of two primary types: multimode fiber and single-mode fiber. Multimode fiber allows multiple light modes to propagate simultaneously, making it ideal for short-distance communication within vehicles, such as data transfer between components. In contrast, single-mode fiber supports a single light mode, enabling higher bandwidth and longer distances, suitable for advanced applications like autonomous driving and vehicle-to-everything (V2X) communication. Both types enhance data transmission efficiency and support growing automotive connectivity demands.</p></p>
