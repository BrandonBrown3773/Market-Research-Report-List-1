<p><ul><li>Wafer Edge Grinder</li><li>Wafer Surface Grinder</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The wafer grinding equipment market includes key types such as wafer edge grinders and wafer surface grinders. Wafer edge grinders focus on refining the periphery of wafers to ensure precise dimensions and prevent chipping, which is crucial for semiconductor manufacturing. Wafer surface grinders, on the other hand, are designed to achieve a smooth and flat surface, enhancing wafer performance in applications like integrated circuits. Both types are essential for improving yield and efficiency in the semiconductor production process.</p></p>
