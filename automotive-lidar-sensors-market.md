<p><strong>The Automotive Lidar Sensors Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Solid State LiDAR</li><li>Mechanical LiDAR</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The automotive LiDAR sensors market features two main types: Solid-State LiDAR and Mechanical LiDAR. Solid-State LiDAR utilizes fixed components and electronic scanning to provide a compact, durable design, ideal for automotive applications, while minimizing moving parts. Mechanical LiDAR, on the other hand, employs rotating components to achieve a wider field of view and higher resolution. Despite being larger and more vulnerable to wear, it offers more detailed data, making both types essential for advancing autonomous driving technologies and enhancing vehicle safety.</p></p>
