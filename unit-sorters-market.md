<p><ul><li>Cross-belt Sorters</li><li>Tilt tray Sorters</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Unit sorters encompass various market types, prominently featuring cross-belt sorters and tilt tray sorters. Cross-belt sorters utilize independent belts to transport items across a wide array of industries, ensuring high-speed and accurate sorting for diverse product types. Conversely, tilt tray sorters employ trays that tilt to drop items at desired locations, offering flexibility in handling various sizes and orientations. Both systems are instrumental in enhancing efficiency and accuracy in distribution centers, warehouses, and e-commerce operations.</p></p>
