<p><strong>The Basket Strainers Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Simplex Basket Strainers</li><li>Duplex Basket Strainers</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The basket strainers market is divided into two primary types: simplex and duplex basket strainers. Simplex basket strainers are designed for use in systems with lower flow rates and require periodic maintenance, as they need to be cleaned when the basket is full. In contrast, duplex basket strainers offer continuous operation by utilizing two baskets, allowing one to be cleaned while the other remains in service, making them ideal for critical applications with uninterrupted flow requirements.</p></p>
