<p><p>Handheld Doppler ultrasound systems are categorized into two main types: directional Doppler and non-directional Doppler. Directional Doppler systems provide information about the flow direction and velocity of blood vessels, facilitating accurate diagnosis of cardiovascular conditions. In contrast, non-directional Doppler systems offer a more generalized assessment of blood flow without specifying direction, making them suitable for quick evaluations. Both types are essential in clinical settings for monitoring vascular health and assisting in various medical procedures.</p></p>
