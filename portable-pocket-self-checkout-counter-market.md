Fluorescence detection employs light to identify and analyze items based on their unique fluorescent properties, allowing for rapid and accurate self-checkout processes. In contrast, magnetic detection utilizes magnetic fields to sense item features, often effective for metal-based products. Both technologies aim to enhance convenience and efficiency in self-checkout experiences, catering to diverse consumer needs in retail environments.</p></p>
