<p><strong>The Passport Reader Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>RFID</li><li>Barcode</li><li>OCR</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The passport reader market comprises three main types: RFID, barcode, and OCR technologies. RFID readers utilize radio frequency identification to digitally scan embedded chips in passports, enhancing security and speed. Barcode readers, on the other hand, rely on optical scanning of printed barcodes found on passports for quick data retrieval. Lastly, OCR (Optical Character Recognition) readers convert printed text into digital data, enabling efficient document processing. Each technology offers distinct advantages in terms of speed, accuracy, and security for passport verification.</p></p>
