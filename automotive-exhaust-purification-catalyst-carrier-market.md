<p><p>The automotive exhaust purification catalyst carrier market consists of various types, including honeycomb, Gasoline Particulate Filters (GPF), and Diesel Particulate Filters (DPF). Honeycomb carriers feature a lattice structure that enhances catalytic efficiency by providing a large surface area for reactions. GPFs specifically target particulate matter in gasoline engines, while DPFs are designed for diesel engines, trapping soot and reducing emissions. These carriers play a crucial role in meeting stringent environmental regulations and improving air quality.</p></p>
