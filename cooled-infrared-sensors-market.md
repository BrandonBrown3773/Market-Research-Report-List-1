<p><p>The cooled infrared sensors market is primarily segmented into two types: thermal sensors and quantum sensors. Thermal sensors detect infrared radiation by measuring heat differences, making them suitable for a range of applications, including surveillance and thermal imaging. In contrast, quantum sensors utilize advanced semiconductor materials to achieve high sensitivity and precision, operating at lower temperatures. Both types are essential for applications in military, industrial, and medical fields, offering unique advantages based on specific deployment needs and environmental conditions.</p></p>
