<p><p>The fluorescent bulb market consists of two main types: Compact Fluorescent Bulbs (CFL) and Linear Fluorescent Bulbs (LFL). CFLs are smaller and designed to replace incandescent bulbs, offering energy efficiency and longer lifespan in a compact form, ideal for residential use. LFLs, on the other hand, are longer tubes commonly used in commercial and industrial settings for general lighting. Both types provide significant energy savings and reduced greenhouse gas emissions compared to traditional incandescent lighting.</p></p>
