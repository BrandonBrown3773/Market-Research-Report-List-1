<p><p>The AC Servo Controller market can be categorized into uniaxial and multiaxial types. Uniaxial controllers manage a single axis of movement, making them ideal for applications requiring precise control in a singular direction, such as robotics or CNC machines. In contrast, multiaxial controllers handle multiple axes, enabling complex movements and coordinated actions, which are essential in advanced automation systems, packaging machinery, and 3D printing. Each type serves distinct industries based on their operational needs and complexity.</p></p>
