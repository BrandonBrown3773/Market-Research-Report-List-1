<p><strong>The High-end ICU Ventilators Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Invasive Ventilation</li><li>Non-invasive Ventilation</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The high-end ICU ventilators market is divided into two main types: invasive ventilation and non-invasive ventilation. Invasive ventilation involves the use of endotracheal tubes or tracheostomy to securely deliver oxygen directly into the lungs, making it suitable for critically ill patients requiring intensive respiratory support. In contrast, non-invasive ventilation employs masks or nasal interfaces to assist breathing without intubation, offering a less intrusive option for patients with respiratory distress while minimizing complications and enhancing comfort.</p></p>
