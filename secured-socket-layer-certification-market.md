<p><p>The Secured Socket Layer (SSL) certification market encompasses three main types: Domain Validation (DV), Organization Validation (OV), and Extended Validation (EV) SSL certificates. DV certificates provide basic encryption after verifying domain ownership, making them suitable for small websites. OV certificates offer higher trust as they require organization verification, ideal for businesses. EV certificates deliver the highest level of security and trust by validating the business and displaying a green address bar, essential for e-commerce and sensitive transactions.</p></p>
