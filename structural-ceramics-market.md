<p><strong>The Structural Ceramics Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Oxide Ceramics</li><li>Non-Oxide Ceramics</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The structural ceramics market is broadly categorized into oxide and non-oxide ceramics. Oxide ceramics, primarily composed of materials like alumina and zirconia, exhibit excellent thermal stability, corrosion resistance, and mechanical strength, making them suitable for applications in aerospace, electronics, and biomedicine. Non-oxide ceramics, such as silicon carbide and boron nitride, are characterized by their high hardness, wear resistance, and thermal conductivity, often used in industrial components, cutting tools, and high-temperature applications. Both types play critical roles in advancing technological innovations.</p></p>
