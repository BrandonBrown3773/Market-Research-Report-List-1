<p><p>The Human Apolipoprotein Test Kits market comprises various types designed to measure specific apolipoproteins, which are critical for lipid metabolism and cardiovascular health. Apolipoprotein A1 Kits assess the primary protein component of HDL cholesterol, important for cardiovascular protection. Apolipoprotein B Kits measure the main protein in LDL cholesterol, related to atherosclerosis risk. Apolipoprotein E Kits evaluate genetic variants affecting cholesterol processing.
