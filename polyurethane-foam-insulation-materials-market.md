<p><p>The polyurethane foam insulation materials market comprises several types, each serving distinct applications. Flexible polyurethane foam (FDF) is widely used in furniture and bedding due to its softness and durability. Semi-flexible polyurethane foam offers moderate firmness, ideal for automotive and packaging applications. Rigid polyurethane foam (RPF) provides superior thermal insulation, commonly used in buildings and refrigeration. Spray polyurethane foam (SPF) is versatile, allowing seamless application on irregular surfaces, making it popular for energy-efficient building insulation and air sealing.</p></p>
