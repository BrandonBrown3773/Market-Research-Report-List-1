<p><p>The chlorine stabilizer market features two primary types: cyanuric acid tablets and cyanuric acid granular. Cyanuric acid tablets provide a slow-dissolving option that is convenient for maintaining stabilizer levels in swimming pools, offering consistent protection against UV degradation of chlorine. In contrast, cyanuric acid granular is a faster-dissolving form, allowing for quick adjustments to stabilizer levels, making it suitable for immediate needs. Both forms help enhance chlorine efficiency by preventing its breakdown from sunlight, ensuring effective sanitation.</p></p>
