<p><p>The OTC test device market includes various testing methods for rapid diagnostics. Lateral flow assays utilize capillary action to produce results quickly, commonly seen in pregnancy tests. Immunoassays leverage antigen-antibody interactions to detect specific substances, often used for biomarkers. Dipstick tests involve inserting a strip into a sample, providing visual results for parameters like glucose or pH. Each method offers convenience, speed, and simplicity, catering to consumer demand for accessible health monitoring tools at home.</p></p>
