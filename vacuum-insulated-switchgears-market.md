<p><p>Vacuum-insulated switchgears (VIS) are critical components in electrical distribution systems, categorized into primary and secondary switchgear markets. Primary switchgear operates at high voltage levels and is essential for transmission networks, providing protection and control. Conversely, secondary switchgear functions at lower voltage levels, typically associated with distribution systems, ensuring power delivery to end-users. Both types utilize vacuum technology to ensure reliability, safety, and space efficiency, making them suitable for various applications in urban and industrial settings.</p></p>
