<p><p>The Polyvinyl Butyral (PVB) resin market is primarily segmented into two types: powder and granule. Powder PVB resin is finely ground and offers superior dispersibility, making it ideal for applications like coatings and adhesives. In contrast, granule PVB resin features larger particle sizes, enhancing handling and processing efficiency, particularly in the production of laminated safety glass. Both types serve distinct needs within various industries, contributing to their growing demand in automotive, construction, and consumer goods applications.</p></p>
