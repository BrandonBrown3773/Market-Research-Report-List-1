Anhydrous Magnesium Sulphate, often utilized in industrial applications, is valued for its moisture-absorbing capabilities. Monohydrate (Kieserite) primarily serves in agricultural and chemical sectors due to its nutrient content, fulfilling essential roles in soil conditioning and nutrient supply. Each type caters to distinct market needs.</p></p>
