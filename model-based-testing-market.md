<p><p>Model Based Testing (MBT) encompasses two primary market types: online and offline testing. Online testing involves real-time evaluation of software applications, allowing for immediate feedback and bug detection. It typically utilizes cloud-based tools to simulate user interactions in a dynamic environment. In contrast, offline testing focuses on pre-recorded test models, which are executed independently of the live system. This approach is beneficial for validating complex scenarios without impacting the operational environment, ensuring thorough analysis and reliability before deployment.</p></p>
