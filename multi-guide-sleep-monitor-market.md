<p><ul><li>EGG Monitor</li><li>Dynamic Monitor</li><li>Video Monitor</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The Multi Guide Sleep Monitor market encompasses three primary types: EGG monitors, dynamic monitors, and video monitors. EGG monitors utilize electroencephalography to track brainwave activity during sleep, providing insights into sleep stages. Dynamic monitors analyze physiological parameters such as heart rate and respiration patterns, offering a comprehensive view of sleep quality. Video monitors capture visual data to observe sleep behaviors and movements, facilitating the identification of sleep disorders. Together, these technologies enhance the understanding and management of sleep health.</p></p>
